About Us

Reshaping How Mass Tort
Claims Are Managed & Defended

Cook Group Analytics was born from a simple realization: the software used to manage mass tort defense was never built for it. Off-the-shelf solutions force firms to juggle multiple disconnected systems, each with its own database, each adding friction to every task.

We built something different. After six years of real-world use at The Cook Group Law Firm, our platform — The Cook Book — has proven that purpose-built technology can transform efficiency, accuracy, and cost management at every level of litigation defense.

Proven in Production

Six years of live use with over one million billable entries processed.

Defense-First Design

Built exclusively for the defense side of mass tort litigation.

Our Vision

To provide the most modern and sophisticated data and technology platforms for anyone involved in the defense of mass tort claims. We use technology to level the playing field, delivering efficiency and precision where it matters most.

The Platform

The Cook Book

One unified platform replacing the patchwork of disconnected tools. Billing, case management, and document management sit on a single database — working in unison in ways that fragmented systems simply cannot.

The Problem

Mass tort cases aren't like other litigation

A single lawsuit can involve dozens of defendants, each with multiple insurance policies, creating exponential complexity. Traditional legal software — built for simple plaintiff-vs-defendant cases — forces firms to maintain separate systems for billing, case management, and documents, each on its own isolated database.
